Quality: About This Data Set

Write a brief summary about the quality of the data set here

Is the data complete?

The data appears to be relatively complete. The study was never interrupted and none of the data appears to have been deleted.

Is the data coherent?

Locations: The dates and times are slightly off. This is likely due to issues converting from UNIX time to a human readable timestamp. This does manifest itself in the visualizations. Changes in the timestamp will alter on which day certain activities took place. Additionally, there is an error in the categoriztion and "Out with friends" appears as a seperate category than "Out with Friends". These are the same thing and should be together.
Screen: The screen data is less coherent. Distinguishing between the different types of phone usage is not straight forward.

Is the data correct?

Due to the timestamp issue, the location data may not be exactly correct. Additionally, the screen data appears to have anomalies as well. For example, the phone being simultaneously locked and unlocked at the same time and date. This must be some collection error and is one of the reasons I chose not to evaluate the screen data here.
